I CARE, INC.

Bridging Paths for Senior Mobility: Your Journey, Our Care

My role: User research, UI/UX design, Prototype & Validation

Team: Monisha Shukla, Haley Hetrick & Sara Howard

Timeline: 3 Weeks

THE CONTEXT

I CARE is a nonprofit that provides free transportation for fixed/low-income seniors throughout DeKalb County to medical appointments and grocery stores. I CARE provides this service with the help of volunteer drivers, who use their own vehicles and gas to transport clients, and who provide caring companionship in our door-to-door service.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Redesigning the website for the non-profit, I care ( Volunteer driver for Seniors). We fell in love with this organization's mission and felt they could use a website refresh as old design is unintuitive.

DESIGN PROCESS

Empathizing with the Users

USER REARCH

So the first step of our research was to identify who are the users for this non profit.

The main goal of the research was to find out what problem were the seniors facing to use this service and the problems faced by the volunteers to sign up for the service.

We aimed to Simplify the Senior Support Volunteer Engagement.
